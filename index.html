Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer pet adoption or only sales?

We currently specialize in selling healthy and well-cared-for pets, including dogs, cats, and more. However, we encourage responsible pet ownership and can guide you to trusted adoption centers if you're interested in adopting a pet.

What health guarantees do you provide for pets?

All our pets undergo regular veterinary check-ups and come with a health certificate. We ensure they are vaccinated and dewormed before they join your family. If you have any concerns after purchase, we offer a limited health guarantee.

Do you provide pet supplies and accessories?

Yes! In addition to selling pets, we also offer a range of pet supplies, including food, toys, grooming products, and accessories to keep your furry friend happy and healthy.
